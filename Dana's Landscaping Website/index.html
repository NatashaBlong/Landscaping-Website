Landscaping Services

Service Benefits
Sodding
  • Instant results
  • Quick erosion control
  • Better weed control
  • Higher cost than seed
  • Must be watered post-installation
Seeding
  • Lower initial cost
  • Longer time to establish erosion control and visible greenery
  • Involves increased risk of weed infestation
  • Must be watered post-installation
Dethatching
(Power Raking)
  • Removal of dead grass from lawn
  • Can be performed with a rake or dethatching machine
  • Allows better access of fertilizer to grass roots
  • Decreased soil compaction to promote grass root growth
  • Done in early spring or late fall to reduce risk of lawn damage
Aerating
  • Creates holes in lawn to reduce soil compaction
  • Increases air, water and nutrient penetration to roots
  • Performed in early spring/fall
