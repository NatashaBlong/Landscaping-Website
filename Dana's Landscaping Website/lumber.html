Lumber

"In the woods we return to reason and faith." - R. Emerson

Trees bless us with fresh air, shade and beauty, but they require occasional care to maintain their health. Dana Blong, founder of Dana's Landscaping & Lawn Care, has 45+ years of experience caring for and cutting down trees. Whether your trees need just a trim, or major reconstruction, Dana's Landscaping is ready to help.

As an arborist who has dedicated his life to Landscaping and tree work, Dana likes to see the wood from the trees he cut serve a second life as furniture, lumber or even firewood. Dana cuts down a variety of tree types, supplying plenty of fresh and dried wood. If you are looking for live-edge lumber for a project or firewood for your family gathering, Dana has you covered.
